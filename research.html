Research Thrusts

My research interests can broadly be classified into following three categories: 1) Power System Optimisation (Energy Resource Management, Distributed Optimisation, Security and Contingency Analysis, Electric Vehicle Scheduling, Non-intrusive Load Monitoring, Differential Privacy); 2) Power System Economics (Optimal Power Flow, Energy Market, Demand Response, Incentive Allocations, Energy Arbitrage); 3) System Engineering (Hardware-in-loop, RTDS, IoT, Cloud Computing, Raspberry Pi, Energy Meter, Sensors, Switches).
