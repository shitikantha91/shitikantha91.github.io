National University of Singapore, Singapore

Postdoctoral Research Fellow
Mar, 2023 - Present

At NUS, initially, my work revolved around the development of a multi-agent energy management framework, aimed at enabling efficient and secure energy trading among Singapore's newly established port terminals. This project received funding from the Energy Market Authority (EMA) of Singapore, and is being practically implemented in collaboration with Envision Digital.

Now, I am working towards developing an intelligent energy management module for Electric Vehicle and Grid cooperation, soon to be implemented by STRIDES for its EV fleet in two new charging stations. Here, we are mobilizing EV fleets to address issues related to uncertain arrivals and peak demand in commercial buildings, while also exploring opportunities for demand response and energy arbitrage. This projects is also sponsored by the Energy Market Authority (EMA) of Singapore.
